About Black Dawn Horizon

Black Dawn Horizon is a free browser-based zombie survival game where every decision shapes your journey. Build your survivor by choosing a weapon, a companion, and a keepsake. Travel the dangerous highways or the quiet backroads, uncover hidden events, and discover not just whether you survive—but who you become.

Every run is written into your survivor's journal: a choice-based, text-driven story of the post-apocalypse where who you help and who you leave behind decides how your story ends. No downloads, no accounts, and nothing to pay—the game runs entirely in your browser. Each playthrough is different, so open the journal, draft your survivor, and see how far the road takes you.
